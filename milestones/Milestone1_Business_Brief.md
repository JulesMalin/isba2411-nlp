# Assignment 1 · Milestone 1 — Business Brief
**ISBA 2411 · Due Week 1 · Individual (used to form teams) · 400–600 words**

Frame your proposed final team project as a short business brief. Submit as PDF via Camino.

## 1. Problem
What real-world NLP problem are you addressing? Who has it?

## 2. Target user / organization
Who is the user or stakeholder, and in what context?

## 3. Value proposition
Why does solving this matter? What's the business or social value?

## 4. NLP hypothesis
Which NLP capability (classification, retrieval/RAG, summarization, extraction, clustering) could
address it, and why?

## 5. Risks & governance
Key risks (bias, privacy, hallucination, misuse) and how you'd begin to mitigate them.

---
*The problem you define here is the one your team carries through every later milestone.*
